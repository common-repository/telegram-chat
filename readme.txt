== Description ==
Do not lose communication with your customers, take all your conversations to your phone by Telegram. Just configure your phone number and ready! Now supports any visitor or information from your phone at any time. It is the easiest way to support and maintain contact with your customers.

If you add more than one phone, you can answer from several cell phone at a time to the same customer, this makes communication more efficient and timely.

If your customers do not want to be connected to your website to have support, invite your client Telegram group and that they also enjoy the ease of communication. All with a single click.

Telegram Chat is the perfect tool for any business whether small or medium enterprises. This is a plug-in fully functional live chat. Increases the conversion rate by direct communication with your visitors using chat Telegram.

Quick View
Once the plugin is activated telegram chat, click "Telegram Chat" in the navigation menu on the left. As soon as a user accesses your site, your data will be displayed in the control panel telegram chat. You must complete all the data and store the information that is asked. Once you complete the form you can already use Telegram Chat



== Installation ==
- Upload the entire Telegram-chat folder to the /wp-content/plugins/ directory.
- Activate the plugin through the \'Plugins\' menu in WordPress.
You will find \'Telegra-chat\' menu in your WordPress admin panel.
